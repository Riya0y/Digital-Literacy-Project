Prevention Tips for Students
✅ Do’s
.Always check the sender’s email address carefully
.Type website URLs manually instead of clicking links
.Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts
.Use strong and unique passwords
.Keep your device and apps updated
❌ Don’ts
.Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages
.Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone
.Avoid downloading attachments from unknown sources
.Do not trust messages that create panic or urgency
.Don’t log in to accounts on public or shared devices
💳 Extra Safety (Financial Protection)
.Always double-check banking messages before responding
.Do not enter banking details on unknown websites
📢 Reporting in Case of Cybercrime
.If you become a victim of phishing:
.Report on the National Cyber Crime Portal
.Call the helpline number: 1930
Taking quick action can help prevent further damage.
