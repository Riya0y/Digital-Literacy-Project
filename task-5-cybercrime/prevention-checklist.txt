Stay Safe Online – Checklist for Students

-   Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp
    messages
-   Always verify the sender before sharing personal information
-   Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
-   Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
-   Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
-   (UPI Safety) Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
-   (UPI Safety) Do not approve unknown payment requests or “Collect
    Requests”
-   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments
-   Download apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store
-   Log out from shared or public computers

Report Cybercrime Immediately: National Cyber Crime Portal:
https://cybercrime.gov.in Helpline Number: 1930
