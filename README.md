## About the Project 

This project was done as part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal. The main aim was to understand how we can use digital tools properly, stay safe online, and build a professional presence.

While doing this project, I got to explore different platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank, and also learned how to communicate professionally through emails and social media.

## What I Have Done

**Task 1 – Infographic**

I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. It explains basic concepts like safe internet usage, useful tools for students, and why digital literacy is important.

**Task 2 – Digital Portfolio**

I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This helped me understand how to maintain a professional online identity.

**Task 3 – Platforms**

I solved a beginner-level problem on HackerRank. I also created a Google Form quiz related to digital literacy and checked the responses using Google Sheets.

**Task 4 – Email & Etiquette**

I wrote two professional emails — one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying to an internship. I also made a simple checklist for social media do’s and don’ts.

**Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness**

I worked on a case study about UPI fraud and learned how such scams happen. I also created a checklist to help students stay safe online.


## What I Learned

This project helped me understand how important digital literacy is in our daily life. I learned how to use different online platforms, communicate professionally, and stay aware of cyber threats. I will continue to use these skills in my academic journey.
